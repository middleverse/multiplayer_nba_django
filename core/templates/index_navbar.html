HOW TO PLAY & FAQ

Welcome to Trivia Splash! A trivia game that tests you on your NBA knowledge. You can play single player, or you can play multiplayer with up to 4 people in a game, including you.


CREATING A GAME

➛ On the home page, click on "Create Game"

➛ Creating a game makes you the Captain of the court, you're in charge of selecting all game configuration options


JOINING A GAME

➛ On the home page, click on "Join Game"

➛ You can join a game by entering the a Court ID (also known as Game ID) and click on "Go"

➛ Court ID has to be provided by the Captain, a friend that has created a Game/Court

➛ Once you join a Game, you'll be assigned the role of a Player


RULES

➛ Each Game has 1 Captain and up to 3 other Players

➛ The Captain is a Player as well but is the only person that is able to configure game options

➛ Each Player is assigned a color, which will become your Player color throughout the game

➛ The Captain decides to choose NBA division(s) which will be the related knowledge you will be tested on and a Shot Clock time, which is the amount of time given to answer each question

➛ Players can see a list of NBA teams included in each division by clicking "List Teams" in the Lobby or in the Create Court page for the Captain

➛ Each game is composed of a varying amount of rounds (at least 15-20 rounds per division)

➛ The number of rounds is based on the total amount of questions in the database for the selected Teams/Divisions

➛ Each round is 10 questions

➛ You can't stop the game until the round has ended, which means all 10 questions have been answered

➛ At the end of each round, the Captain has the option of changing these Game Config Options, but not before that

➛ You have until the Shot Clock duration to answer each question

➛ The Shot Clock resets after every question

➛ You're able to see how many friends have posted their answers for the current question by looking at the number of basketballs beside "Answers Posted"

➛ Each correct answer is 1 point, and an incorrect answer is 0 points

➛ At the end the round, you'll be presented with the results, only the correct answers are marked (not to be confused with what each player answered)


FAQs

➛ Max Players: 4

➛ Shot Clock: 17 or 24 seconds

➛ Questions Per Round: 10

➛ Divisions: Official NBA List
